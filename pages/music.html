Music

Nobuo’s compositions are very diverse in styles, ranging from classical symphonic pieces, heavy metal, new-age and hyper-percussive techno-electronica. Nobuo has disclosed that his work is also influenced by Celtic and Irish music as he considers himself a big fan of these styles. Feel free to listen to some of his work and don't forget to share it with your friends.
